 The papers are titled “Logic-Based Verification of Task Allocation for LLM-Enabled Multi-Agent Manufacturing Systems” and “Attack-Resilient Control of Finite-State Cyber-Physical Systems via Inspection States." Congrats Jonghan and Mostafa!
