Dr. Kovalenko has has received support through the Penn State Institute for Computational and Data Sciences (ICDS) Mid-Scale Seed Grant Program.

The project “Water Evaluation and Treatment Digital Twin (WET-DT): A Framework for Robust, Predictive Process Controls for Water Treatment Plants,” was selected as part of a new ICDS initiative supporting interdisciplinary research across the University. The project brings together collaborators from University Park and Penn State Behrend and contributes to ICDS research in digital twins and computational/data-driven science.
